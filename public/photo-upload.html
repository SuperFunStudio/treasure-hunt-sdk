💡 Professional Photo Tips

💡

Lighting

Use natural light or bright indoor lighting. Avoid flash and harsh shadows.

📐

Angles

Show front, back, sides, and any defects. Include close-ups of important details.

🎯

Focus

Ensure photos are sharp and clear. Blurry photos reduce buyer confidence.

🖼️

Background

Use a clean, neutral background. White or light gray works best for most items.

🔍

Details

Include photos of tags, labels, serial numbers, and any wear or damage.

📏

Scale

Show size by including common objects (coin, ruler) or wearing/using the item.
